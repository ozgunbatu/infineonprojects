Limitations & Caveats

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Limitations

  • No Access to Internal Data: Actual customer contracts, confidential pricing agreements, and detailed cost structures are unavailable. All pricing estimates are based on public sources and industry benchmarks.
  • Simulated Customer Insights: Customer interviews and feature prioritization scores are based on public statements, industry reports, and reasonable assumptions rather than direct customer feedback.
  • Regional Variance Not Fully Modeled: Pricing and market dynamics vary significantly by region (EMEA vs. APAC vs. Americas). This analysis focuses primarily on EMEA market.
  • Competitive Response Not Modeled: Analysis assumes static competitor positioning. In reality, TI, Analog, and NXP would likely respond with product launches or pricing actions.
  • Technology Risk: ASIL-D certification timeline and cost are estimates. Actual validation could take longer or require additional investment.
  • Market Adoption Uncertainty: 48V and ADAS L2+ penetration rates are based on industry forecasts that may not materialize (e.g., regulatory changes, economic downturn).
